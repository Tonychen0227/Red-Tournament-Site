Ascension Swiss Format

This year, we'll be using running a new format called Ascension Swiss.

All participants will take part in three rounds in the Group Stage.

There are two pools for the Swiss Rounds: Normal and Ascension. All players start in the Normal Pool in the first round, with matches being drawn completely randomly.

Winning your match in the Normal Pool in any round will advance you to the Ascension Pool for the next round, if applicable.

Winning your match in the Ascension Pool advances you straight to the Quarterfinals. Losing your match (coming in last place) will drop you down back to the Normal Pool for the next round, if applicable.

In addition to pool advancements, points are earned after each match depending on standing and pool.

Playoffs Format

After the Group Stage, the top 27 players will compete in a single-elimination winner-takes-all bracket consisting of the Quarter-Finals (27 players), Semi-Finals (9 players), and Finals (3 players).

Estimated tournament runtime:

Ascension Swiss Scoring

Each round in the Group Stage will see all competitors be matched in a 3-player race with other competitors from their same bracket. There may be some 2-player races possible due to bracket imbalances, but will be minimized where possible.

Round 1 Points Distribution

  • Winner: 10 points, advancement to Ascension Pool
  • Middle*: 9 points, remain in Normal Pool
  • Bottom: 6 points, remain in Normal Pool

* Only applies to 2nd place in 3-player races. Coming in 2nd in a 2-player race is considered bottom position

Round 2 Points Distribution

Pool Winner Middle* Bottom
Ascension Pool Earned BYE (straight to Quarter-Finals and Special Exhibition Match) 10 points, remain in Ascension Pool 6 points, demoted to Normal Pool
Normal Pool 10 points, advancement to Ascension Pool 6 points, remain in Normal Pool 3 points, remain in Normal Pool

* Only applies to 2nd place in 3-player races. Coming in 2nd in a 2-player race is considered bottom position
